About Me

I grew up on the beautiful island of St. Croix, in the U.S. Virgin Islands. I bet you wish it was that much fun to go visit your mom. I have lived in New York City for the past 15 years, and just moved to Los Angeles a few months ago. West Coast culture shock! Where are all the good bagels? Why must I drive?

I have worked as a web developer in different capacities for many years, both as a freelancer and on large development teams. When I finish Coding Boot Camp, my dream is to either join or start a worker-owned technology cooperative! I also draw comics, make puppets, and am currently working on a stop-motion animation for a Halloween video show. I hope that I can work my two jobs, finish my animation, and keep up with my coding boot camp homework!
